Music

PROFOUND LOVE FOR MUSIC

HOW DID IT START?

Music has been the biggest part of my life ever since I was a child. Throughout my life, I've been a fan of many genres and it has helped me to understand music itself better. First I began with writing casual poems that turned into lyrics without any melody. As I grew older, I started looking for more ways to create music.

Guitar playing

2013 - 2018

Prior to year 2013, I was listening to pop music. But that year, I discovered an underground genre called grunge that changed my entire perspective when it comes to music. My favorite grunge band is Alice in Chains - it inspired me to buy a big collection of CDs which I still own to this day, and the band's guitarist inspired me to buy a guitar and learn how to play it. After three solid years of listening to grunge and practising everyday, I switched to metal music and gradually stopped practising.

HIP HOP

2018 - ∞

It took me a long time to find a genre that fits every criteria that I have in order to enjoy music. I mainly listen to old school east coast hip hop artists. Some of my favorites are The Artifacts, The Notorious B.I.G., Big L, Mobb Deep and Heather B. Recently, I started researching trap music and even implementing trap elements into my work.

FL STUDIO

2019-∞

Even though I am relatively new at producing, I enjoy the craft a lot. Songwriting would definitely be my ideal career if I could make a living out of it. I use software called FL Studio, through which I make my beats. I use the SP-1200 plugin for all of my projects since I mainly create old school hip hop beats, but I occassionally make a remix of trap songs.
